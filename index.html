About Aeroclub Larissa

For over 25 years, Aeroclub Larissa has been at the forefront of aviation training and services in Central Greece. Founded by passionate aviators, we have trained hundreds of pilots and continue to maintain the highest standards of safety and excellence.

Our experienced team of certified flight instructors and maintenance professionals are dedicated to providing world-class aviation services in a safe, professional, and friendly environment.
